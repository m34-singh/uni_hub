Data Protection

At Uni Hub, we are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This statement outlines what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and how we ensure your data is protected.

1. What Information We Collect

When you register and use Uni Hub, we collect the following personal data:

  • Identity Information:
    Full Name
    Username
    Email Address
    Password (securely stored using encryption)
    Role (Admin, Community Leader, Events Leader, or User)
  • Educational & Demographic Information:
    Course
    Age
    Start Date
  • Profile and Preferences:
    Interests
    Date Joined
    Active Status (used to manage account access)

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is collected and used for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To assign appropriate roles and permissions within the Uni Hub platform.
  • To personalize your experience and connect you with relevant communities and events.
  • To communicate updates and important information related to your Uni Hub membership.
  • To maintain platform security and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily provide your information during registration.
  • Contractual Necessity: To deliver the services and features of Uni Hub.
  • Legitimate Interest: For internal analytics, role-based access, and community engagement.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are securely hashed before storage.
  • Access controls are in place to restrict who can view or edit personal information.
  • All data transmissions are encrypted using secure protocols.

5. Data Retention

Your data will be retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion.
  • Object to or restrict processing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority.

7. Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding your data, or to exercise your rights, please contact:

Email: support@unihub.com
